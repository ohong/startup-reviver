# Contrary Research Report Format

## Overview Structure

```
# [Company Name]

[Tags/Industry Categories]

#### Key Info
- Founding Date: [Date]
- Headquarters: [Location]
- Total Funding: [Amount]
- Status: [Private/Public/Acquired]
- Stage: [Seed/Series A/etc]
- Employees: [Number]

##### Reading Time
[X] min

---

## Memo

### Thesis
[2-3 paragraph thesis about the market, problem, and company's solution]

### Founding Story
[Detailed founding story with sources]

### Product
[Detailed product description including:]
- Product overview
- Key features and capabilities
- Product lines/segments (if applicable)
- Technical architecture highlights
- Use cases

### Market
[Market analysis including:]
- Market size and dynamics
- Market trends
- Customer segments
- Market positioning

### Competition
[Competitive landscape:]
- Direct competitors
- Indirect competitors
- Competitive advantages/disadvantages
- Market share where available

### Business Model
[Revenue model, pricing, unit economics]

### Traction
[Customer metrics, growth, key milestones]

### Valuation
[Funding rounds, valuations, investors]

### Key Opportunities
[Future growth opportunities, market trends, technology shifts]

### Key Risks
[Competitive, market, execution, technology risks]

### Summary
[2-3 paragraph executive summary]

---

## Authors

[Author information]

## Resources

[List of resources - articles, interviews, etc.]

## Similar Companies

[Related companies in the same space]
